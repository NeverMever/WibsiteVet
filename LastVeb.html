About Our Clinic

VetCare is a team of professionals who truly love animals and do their best for their health and well-being.

We offer a comprehensive approach to your pet's health

Our clinic is equipped with modern equipment for accurate diagnostics and effective treatment. We work with animals of all types and ages, from small hamsters to large dogs.

Our specialists regularly improve their qualifications by participating in international conferences and seminars. We follow the latest achievements in veterinary medicine and implement them in our practice.

VetCare has a comfortable atmosphere that reduces stress in animals. We understand that a visit to the vet can be exciting, so we do everything to make your pet feel calm and safe.
